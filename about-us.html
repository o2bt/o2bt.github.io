What we're all about

Simple. O2BT is computer science tutorials made by students for students. We make videos that we think are practical, useful and easy to follow. Most importantly, we make them for our peers, i.e. fellow budding nerds and hackers. Because knowledge is a cool thing and we like sharing cool things.
